IoT-Based Patient Assistance System

A revolutionary device for assisting elderly patients with medication adherence.

Overview

The IoT-Based Patient Assistance System is an innovative solution designed to help elderly patients manage their medications effectively. By combining automated reminders, accurate pill dispensing, and real-time monitoring, this system ensures that patients take the correct medication on time.

Key Features

  • Uses a stepper motor-driven rotary dispenser to provide the correct dosage at scheduled times.

  • Connected to a mobile app for reminders, ensuring patients don’t miss any doses.

  • Equipped with an LCD display that shows dosage history, schedules, and more.

  • Allows caregivers to monitor patient adherence and adjust medication schedules through the app.

  • Ensures secure storage and handling of medications, protecting patient information and privacy.

Benefits

This system provides several advantages to patients and caregivers:

  • Reduces dependence on caregivers for medication management.
  • Ensures accurate, timely medication intake, minimizing missed doses.
  • Promotes independence, helping elderly patients maintain their health and autonomy.

Technical Specifications

The system is built on an ESP32 microcontroller with built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth capabilities, allowing seamless communication with a connected mobile app. It uses a 28BYJ-48 stepper motor for precise medication dispensing and a ULN2003 motor driver for stable performance. Power is supplied by a long-lasting lithium-ion battery, ensuring continuous operation.
